About us

Our Travel Agency is a licensed Tour operator established in 2000 and located in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria.
We have researched destinations and Hotels, and can design a program providing the best possible value within our client’s budget. We are committed to provide services and to ease the travel plans of businessmen and tourists, alike giving our clients peace of mind and enabling them to relax and enjoy their trip. We offer a range of quality services to aid and inspire travelers at home. Some of our tour activities include: hotel reservations, transfers, car rental, flight and bus tickets, business trips, Eco, Historical, Cultural, Heritage, Wine – testing, Hunting and Sightseeing Tours, professional tour guides etc. In addition to accommodating Individual travelers, Travel Agency provides excellent and innovative Group travel programs, Winter, Summer and Spa vacations.
